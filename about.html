ASPA

Motto: Lead with heart, Serve with Purpose
(Corde Ducimus, Proposito Servimus)

ASPA Vision

To be a dynamic student organization that nurtures future public administrators who embody leadership, service, and excellence in nation-building.

ASPA Mission

The Association for Students of Public Administration (ASPA) is dedicated to:

  1. Inspire students to pursue excellence in governance and public service.
  2. Cultivate leadership skills rooted in integrity, inclusivity, and accountability.
  3. Engage members in programs and initiatives that respond to community and societal needs.
  4. Uphold the values of responsible citizenship and ethical governance.
